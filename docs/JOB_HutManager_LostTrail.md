**Location:** Coldstream Canyon, CA (near Truckee)

---

## Who We Are

Lost Trail Lodge is a backcountry lodge in Coldstream Canyon, tucked into the Sierra Nevada a short distance from Truckee. We've recently taken over operations from our former operator and are rebuilding this property as an independently run mountain retreat — open to serious outdoor people, local community members, and groups who want a real backcountry experience without the BS.

We're not a hotel. We're not a glamping resort. We're a working lodge in a canyon, wood stove and all, with a riverside sauna, a full kitchen, and real mountain terrain out the front door. In winter, that means snowmobile or ski-in access, quiet, and exactly the kind of place you'd want to live if you love the mountains.

We're looking for someone who already lives that life and wants to get paid to share it.

---

## The Role

You're the face of Lost Trail during the winter months. You'll live on-site (private accommodations provided) and serve as host, caretaker, logistics coordinator, and first point of contact for guests. The property runs lean — this isn't a housekeeping or concierge role in a traditional sense. Guests are expected to pull their weight. You're there to make sure they can.

**Core responsibilities:**

- Welcome arriving guests, walk them through the property
- Ensure all systems are operational: wood stove, generator, water system, sauna
- Light the sauna, check firewood supply, keep the property functional
- Coordinate guest add-ons: connecting groups with guides, chefs, snowmobile rentals, and cat skiing partners
- Oversee laundry changeovers between bookings (sheets, towels — guests do basic cleanup, you handle the rest)
- Handle property emergencies: power issues, weather events, guest situations
- Maintain bear-safe practices and help guests understand and follow them
- Manage outdoor camera system (public areas only)
- Light maintenance and upkeep — report anything that needs professional attention
- Be a local resource: trail conditions, snowpack, weather, what's happening in Truckee

**What you're NOT responsible for:**
- Cooking meals for guests (a la carte model — guests bring their own or book a chef)
- Intensive housekeeping (guests are responsible for leaving the property as found)
- Operating as a concierge or full-service hotelier

## Ideal Candidate

- Lives in or near Truckee, or wants to
- Deeply comfortable in backcountry winter conditions — this isn't a cabin in the suburbs
- Experienced with off-grid systems: wood stoves, generators, water systems
- Comfortable with snowmobile operation (training available if needed) or ski touring
- Self-sufficient and calm under pressure — you're the resource when something goes wrong
- Warm and genuine with guests — you don't have to be a people-person hotel host, but you do have to be real and helpful
- Some experience in hospitality, lodging, guiding, or land management preferred but not required
- Comfortable with basic tech: we use a mobile-first booking and guest system, you'll need to be comfortable with it
- Has own vehicle capable of winter driving
